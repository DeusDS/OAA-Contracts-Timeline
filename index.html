Key Findings

Two Critical Points

Finding 1

Consistent April–May Submission History

Subgrantee contract requests have been submitted to OGC every single year in April or May — in SFY23 (Apr 5), SFY24 (May 15), SFY25 (Apr 8), and SFY26 (May 15). This is an average of 123 calendar days before DAS executes the state contract.

Finding 2

Waiting for DAS Would Push Contracts to Nov–Dec+

If submission had waited until DAS executed the master grant, subgrantees would not have fully executed contracts until November–December at the earliest — and in some cases well into the following calendar year. Early submission saves an average of 292 calendar days per fiscal year.
